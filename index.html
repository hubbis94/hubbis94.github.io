Welcome to Children's Charities Tennis Classic!

A Charity Tennis Tournament Running for over 40 years
in New Britain CT

Our 46th annual tournament will be held August 17 and 28, 2024 at the New Britain High School Tennis Courts.
There will be several divisions with kids through adults competing from throughout the region to help raise money for the Hospital of Special Care's Pediatric Renovation Project.

Divisions

This year we will have 17 different divisions, a few more than last year. There will be A and B categories.
A divisions are meant for experienced/varsity players. B divisions are for recreational/jv players.

Singles (experienced)

  • Boys 16 & under singles (A)
  • Boys 18 & under singles (A)
  • Girls 16 & under singles (A)
  • Girls 18 & under singles (A)
  • Grand Masters (Men's Singles 19-25)
  • Men's Singles A (Age 26+)
  • Women's Singles A (age 19+)

    • Singles (recreational)

  • Boys 16 & under singles (B)
  • Boys 18 & under singles (B)
  • Girls 16 & under singles (B)
  • Girls 18 & under singles (B)
  • Men's Singles B (Age 19+)
  • Women's Singles B (age 19+)

    • Doubles (M/W/Mixed)

  • Family Doubles (one player must be 18 & under)
  • 18 & Under Open Doubles
  • Adult Open Doubles A - experienced
  • Adult Open Doubles B - recreational

    Our Mission

    “Success is a journey, not a destination.”

    This quote from Mark Twain really sums up the Children’s Charities Tennis Classic.

    For 45 years, the Children’s Charities Tennis Classic (CCTC) has strived to provide a better and happier life for those youngsters afflicted with illness or injury. Through toys, games, medical equipment, renovation projects and various other needs, we have worked to build a better day for these very special young people.

    As I look back over the journey of these many decades, I believe that our success is derived, in large part, from the support and commitment of people like you. Your commitment as a Sponsor has helped to pave the way to a better life for hundreds of young people throughout the years. Your financial support is key to helping us make a brighter day for so many.

    This year, the 45th CCTC will benefit the Pediatric Nursery Renovation Project at the Hospital for Special Care. This nursery is a very special place – helping those infants with complex medical conditions and severe injuries. The highly skilled staff does much to ensure that these very young patients are given the best of care and treatment during their time of need.

    I am so pleased that we can support this wonderful hospital and its highly regarded infant nursery. So, I ask you, most sincerely, will you partner with me and help us reach our goal of raising $7,000 for this most worthy project?

    I need your support to help continue the success of the CCTC. Your gift will not only help to reach our goal, but more importantly, it will help us to make a young child’s life better.

    The tournament will be held August 19 and 20 at the New Britain High School Tennis Courts and will have divisions from youth through adult. It is my hope that you will consider joining me on this great journey for our youth.

    Sincerely,
    Don Lukowski
    Founder and Chair
